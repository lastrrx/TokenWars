❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How are tokens selected for competitions? +
Our automated algorithm pairs tokens with similar market caps (within ±10%) to ensure fair competition. Tokens must have minimum liquidity, be at least 30 days old, and pass our eligibility criteria.
What happens if I pick the wrong token? +
If your chosen token underperforms, you lose your 0.1 SOL entry fee. This goes into the prize pool for the winners. Remember, only predict what you can afford to lose!
Can I cancel a prediction? +
No, all predictions are final once placed. This ensures fairness for all participants and prevents manipulation of the prize pool.
How fast are payouts? +
Payouts are instant! As soon as the competition ends and winners are determined by TWAP, the smart contract automatically distributes winnings to all successful predictors.
What happens in case of a tie? +
Ties are extremely rare with TWAP pricing calculated to multiple decimal places. In the unlikely event of an exact tie, all participants receive their entry fees back.
Are there any restrictions? +
You must be 18 years or older to participate. TokenWars is not available in jurisdictions where prediction markets are prohibited. Always check your local regulations.
