How TokenWars Works

📊

Smart Token Matching

Our algorithm pairs tokens with similar market caps (±10%) for fair competition. Predict which token will perform better over a specified timeframe.

💰

Fixed Entry Stakes

Simple 0.1 SOL entry fee per prediction. Winners split the pool (minus 15% platform fee). Transparent and straightforward.

🔐

Trustless Settlement

All funds locked in Solana smart contracts. Winners determined by TWAP (Time-Weighted Average Price) for manipulation resistance.

📊 How Predictions Work
Tokens are paired based on similar market caps. You predict which will perform better over 1 hour. Winners are determined by TWAP (Time-Weighted Average Price) to prevent manipulation.
💰 Prize Distribution
  • Entry Fee: 0.1 SOL per prediction
  • Winner's Share: Split the pool with other winners
  • Platform Fee: 15% for operations and development
  • Settlement: Automatic via smart contracts
🔒 Fair Play & Security
All predictions are final once placed. Smart contracts ensure transparent and tamper-proof results. Your wallet remains under your control at all times.
